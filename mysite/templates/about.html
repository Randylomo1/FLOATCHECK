About FloatCheck

FloatCheck is a powerful and intuitive financial reconciliation application designed to help businesses of all sizes streamline their payment processes. Our platform provides a comprehensive suite of tools for managing and verifying transactions, with a special focus on M-Pesa payments.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower businesses with the tools they need to achieve financial clarity and efficiency. We believe that by simplifying the reconciliation process, we can help our clients save time, reduce errors, and make more informed financial decisions.

Our Features

  • Multi-source Ingestion: Import transaction data from a variety of sources, including M-Pesa (SMS and API), bank statements (CSV, OFX), and manual uploads.
  • Automatic Matching Engine: Our highly configurable rule engine automatically matches transactions based on your custom criteria, with support for exact, fuzzy, and time-based matching.
  • Exception Management: Our intuitive dashboard makes it easy to identify and resolve exceptions, with smart suggestions and bulk resolution options.
  • Real-time Reconciliation: With support for webhooks and polling, you can reconcile transactions in real-time and always have an up-to-date view of your finances.
  • Subscription Management: Our flexible subscription plans allow you to choose the level of functionality that's right for your business, with a range of pricing options to fit your budget.
