About FloatCheck

FloatCheck is a streamlined financial management and reconciliation tool built to bring clarity and accuracy to your business's financial records. We empower businesses to easily ingest financial documents, process payments, and compare their internal transaction records against external sources, like bank statements or payment processor reports, to instantly identify discrepancies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate the tedious, error-prone process of manual reconciliation and financial data management. We provide a simple, powerful platform that saves businesses valuable time and resources, prevents financial loss, and offers a clear, verifiable picture of their transaction history.

How It Works

FloatCheck simplifies financial management into a few straightforward steps:

  1. Upload Financial Documents: Securely upload your financial documents in various formats, including CSV, Excel, PDF, and OFX.
  2. Connect Your Systems: Seamlessly integrate with external accounting and ERP systems like QuickBooks, Xero, and Sage.
  3. Automated Reconciliation: Our powerful engine automatically compares thousands of transactions, identifying discrepancies in real-time.
  4. Gain Actionable Insights: Our intuitive dashboard provides a clear overview of your financial activities, helping you make informed decisions.

Core Features

  • Effortless File Ingestion: Upload financial documents in various formats. Our asynchronous processing ensures a smooth and efficient user experience.
  • Business Profile Management: Create and manage your business profile, including industry, contact information, and more.
  • Seamless Integrations: Connect with external accounting and ERP systems. Webhooks provide real-time updates and keep your financial data in sync.
  • M-Pesa Payments: Process M-Pesa payments securely and reliably, with robust validation and error handling.
  • Automated Reconciliation: Define your own rules and let our system handle the rest, saving you time and reducing errors.
  • Insightful Dashboard: Track key metrics, view recent transactions, and monitor the reconciliation process in real-time.
